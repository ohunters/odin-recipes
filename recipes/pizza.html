Classic Homemade Pizza Recipe

Make a delicious homemade pizza with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese. This recipe is simple, customizable, and perfect for any pizza night!

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Sauce:

  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Toppings:

  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
