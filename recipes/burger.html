Classic Cheeseburger Recipe

Enjoy a juicy homemade cheeseburger with a perfectly seasoned patty, melted cheese, and fresh toppings. A simple yet satisfying meal!

Ingredients:

For the Patty:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder

For the Burger:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • ½ onion (sliced)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Patties:
    1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
    2. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into patties slightly larger than the buns.
  2. Cook the Patties:
    1. Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat.
    2. Cook patties for 3–4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
    3. During the last minute, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover until melted.
  3. Toast the Buns:
    1. Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast on the grill or pan until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the Burger:
    1. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by the cheesy patty.
    2. Add tomato slices, onions, pickles, and desired condiments.
    3. Top with the other bun and serve immediately.
